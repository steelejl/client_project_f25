What is STAR?

How do I use it?
  • To guide your interviews

STAR is an acronym for Story, Task, Action and Result. This is a useful tool that helps shape
your interview into succint responses to the questions. You can think of it like you are
a skilled storyteller and each response conveys a specific message that highlights who
are as a professional.
